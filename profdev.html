Professional Development

The UPE Professional Development committee offers a variety of services to help students prepare for the workforce, ranging from resume critiques and mock technical interviews to public workshops covering various stages of the recruiting process and beyond.

Technical Interviews

Interviews for Software Engineering (SWE) positions often include a technical portion, where candidates are expected to solve a coding or design problem. These can range from solving by hand on a whiteboard to solving with an IDE.

The UPE Professional Development committee offers mock technical interviews. To sign up for a mock interview, book a slot on the calendar below at least 24 hours before the appointment time. More information about specific slots are in the description for each appointment.

For more resources, check out the UPE Library for books on interview questions!

Resume Critiques

To receive a personalized resume critique, drop by the office (346 Soda) during office hours (M-F 10AM - 4PM), or send your resume to profdev@upe.berkeley.edu with the title “[UPE] Resume Critique Request”.
